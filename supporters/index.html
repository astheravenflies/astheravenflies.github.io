Supporters

/ Donate

This is a grassroots project. Early on, we made the choice to not be part of major funding projects because we wanted to tell a story that was not corporation- or government-sponsored. We aim to discover and share the diversity and complexity of peoples’ relationships with and within Canada, and seek to honour their voices with honesty. Crowdfunding, therefore, was an important choice to ground our project on the support of everyday Canadians.

On February 1, we launched a crowdfunding campaign that raised over $16,000. We are both humbled and empowered by the support we’ve received, and will update this page with our backers names once we’ve gathered that info.
